What's FixIt™ ?

FixIt™ is a Peer-2-Peer platform that aims at providing public and social housing tenants with a faster and more affordable access to simple housing maintenance services, by linking them with handymen in their area.

How does It Work ?

The concept is quite simple :

  1. Users with handiwork experience can apply to join our team of handymen (whether your skills are basic or advanced, broad or specific, everyone's welcome !).
  2. Users in need of a "quick fix" can submit their request through our dedicated form.
  3. We will then forward their request to our nearby partnered handymen whose skills match the type of service they solicited.
  4. Once the request approved, the solicited task will be delivered by a FixIt handyman.
  5. If we fail to find a FixIt handyman with the skills needed nearby, the user is given the choice of forwarding his request to the nearest and least expensive maintenance company in the area, or cancelling his request.
